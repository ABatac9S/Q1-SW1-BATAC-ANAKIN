Gacha Games - Addicting Past Time

Gacha games are a video game genre that can typically only on laptops, pcs, and phones and have a type of gambling aspect to them. There is a currency system in gacha games that are typically for characters on banners that change depending on when the next update comes out.

Why people play gacha games

Games with a long grind to it doesn't seem very appealing at first but typically that gets people interested is the story of the game and its characters.
Even though it may take weeks to even months to save up for the character on the banner you like people will still grind because of the impact that it had on them to the design or their backstory.
